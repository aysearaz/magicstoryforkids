Privacy Policy

App Name: MagicTales – Magic Story For Kids
Developer: Ayse Araz

This privacy policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects personal data. The app is designed for children and follows the rules of child privacy protection.

1. Data We Collect

Our app may collect the following data:

  • Voice recordings: Used for speech recognition and feedback.
  • Usage statistics: Anonymously collected to improve the app.
  • Device information: For crash reporting and technical analysis.

✅ No personal identification like name, age, or email is collected.

2. How We Use the Data

The collected data is used only to:

  • Provide interactive speech feedback.
  • Personalize stories or exercises.
  • Analyze app usage to improve performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any personal data with third parties.
The app functions offline, and voice data is not stored or transmitted unless explicitly required by the parent.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children and complies with:

  • COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • Google Play Families Policy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

5. Security

We take data security seriously. Any data (if collected) is handled securely and only within the app.
